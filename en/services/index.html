Interpreting & Communication Support

I provide interpreting support for international business settings such as trade fairs, business meetings, factory visits and technical discussions. With hands-on experience in manufacturing and global business, I support smooth communication by understanding not only words, but also the background, context and business intent.

Trade Fairs Product explanations, visitor support, booth assistance and demo interpreting
Business Meetings Communication support for price, delivery schedule, specifications and business negotiations
Technical Discussions Factory visits, equipment explanations, quality-related topics and technical Q&A
Site Visits On-site visits, research meetings and interpreting / support

Pricing Guide

Booth attendant: half day from €200 / full day from €350
Trade fair visit interpreting: half day from €200 / full day from €400
Business meeting, factory visit or technical interpreting: half day from €250 / full day from €450

* Final pricing depends on the subject, field and location. Transportation and accommodation costs are charged separately. For trade fairs, please provide an entrance ticket or exhibitor pass when required.

Overseas Sales & Business Support

I support overseas sales activities for companies without a local base in Europe, including market research, local contact handling, meeting coordination and follow-up for the European market.

Market Research Research on competitors, pricing, sales channels and local market information
Sales Support Local contact handling, meeting coordination and follow-up
Business Partner Support Email communication, schedule coordination and organization of key discussion points
Visits & On-Site Support Visit coordination, on-site accompaniment and brief reporting

Pricing Guide

Hourly rate: from €50 / hour
Monthly support: from €500, quoted depending on scope
Visit accompaniment: half day from €250 / full day from €450

* Final pricing is quoted individually depending on the scope, duration, required support range and deliverables.

Import/Export, Accounting & Admin Support

I provide hands-on support for international transactions between Japan, China and Europe, including import/export coordination, logistics communication, accounting assistance, invoice management and general administration.

Import/Export Support Shipment communication, customs documentation checks and practical coordination related to German/EU requirements
Logistics Coordination Communication with freight forwarders, inventory and shipment status checks, and logistics progress management
Accounting Assistance Monthly closing preparation, accounts payable/receivable checks, invoice management and coordination with tax advisors
Admin Support Contract document management, internal/external coordination and general administration for German operations

Pricing Guide

Hourly rate: from €40 / hour
Ongoing or monthly support: discussed individually depending on scope and working hours

* Final pricing depends on the scope, duration and level of expertise required. Tax or legal decisions should be handled in cooperation with qualified tax advisors or legal professionals.
